# Code of Conduct

## Our commitment

We are committed to a respectful, harassment-free community for everyone,
regardless of experience, identity, background, ability, or viewpoint.

## Expected behavior

- Be respectful, specific, and constructive.
- Discuss evidence and ideas without attacking people.
- Welcome questions and different levels of experience.
- Respect privacy, attribution, licenses, and project boundaries.
- Accept maintainer decisions and corrections gracefully.

## Unacceptable behavior

Harassment, threats, discrimination, sexualized attention, deliberate
intimidation, doxxing, sustained disruption, and publishing another person's
private information without permission are not tolerated. Abusing project
channels to distribute ROMs, credentials, or other unauthorized content is
also unacceptable.

## Reporting and enforcement

Report conduct concerns privately through the repository's
[private reporting form](https://github.com/tsilva/SuperMarioBros-Nes-turbo/security/advisories/new)
and prefix the report title with `CONDUCT:`. Do not open a public issue when a
report identifies or could expose an individual.

The maintainer will review reports confidentially, avoid conflicts of interest
where possible, and may remove content, issue a warning, restrict
participation, or permanently ban a participant. Reports made in bad faith may
receive the same response. Retaliation against a reporter is prohibited.

This code applies in repository spaces and when someone publicly represents
the project.

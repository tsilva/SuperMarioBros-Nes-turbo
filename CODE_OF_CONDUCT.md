# Code of Conduct

## Our commitment

We are committed to a welcoming, harassment-free community for everyone,
regardless of background, identity, experience level, or personal
characteristics. Be respectful, constructive, and considerate in project
spaces.

## Expected behavior

- Discuss ideas and code without attacking people.
- Give actionable feedback and assume good faith while remaining evidence-led.
- Respect privacy, attribution, licenses, and project boundaries.
- Accept maintainer decisions gracefully, even when you disagree.

Harassment, threats, discrimination, sexualized attention, deliberate
disruption, publishing private information, and other conduct that would be
unreasonable in a professional setting are not acceptable.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may edit or remove contributions and may temporarily or permanently
restrict participation when necessary. Report conduct concerns privately to the
maintainer through the contact options on
[the maintainer's GitHub profile](https://github.com/tsilva). Reports will be
handled as confidentially as reasonably possible. Retaliation against a
reporter is prohibited.

This policy applies to repository interactions and to public spaces where a
person is representing the project.
